About Tinsae Tv

TINSAE TV Church is a Christian media platform that broadcasts sermons, worship services, and Bible teachings, mainly in Amharic. It serves Ethiopian believers and the diaspora with live streams, gospel music, and spiritual programs to strengthen faith and spread the Gospel. Affiliated with the Ethiopian Evangelical Church, it provides a virtual church experience for viewers worldwide.
