Who We Are

TINSAE TV Development and Welfare Organization (JCDWO) is a faith-based organization that works to empower and transform the lives of the most vulnerable and marginalized communities in Ethiopia. We are inspired by the vision and values of the TINSAE TV, which was founded by pioneer Finnish missionaries in 1951. We are legally registered and recognized by the Ethiopian government since 1999. We have been implementing various development, humanitarian, and peacebuilding programs in different regions of Ethiopia, reaching over 170,000 beneficiaries. We have also built strong partnerships with local and international stakeholders, such as government agencies, donors, and civil society organizations. We are committed to creating a prosperous and peaceful society, where everyone can enjoy their dignity and rights.
